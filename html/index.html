PEAKY BLINDERS

Peaky Blinders is a British crime TV series primarily set in Birmingham, England in the aftermath of World War I.
The fictional gang is loosely based on the Peaky Blinders, a real urban youth gang who were active in the city from the 1890s and early 20th century.
The dark, dramatic soundtrack is a window into the mind of Tommy - a tortured ex-soldier. Artists such as Nick Cave, Laura Marling, Johnny Cash, the White Stripes have written original songs for the show.
